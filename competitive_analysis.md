## PLATFORM 1: CAVEON

### Overview
Caveon is a test security and psychometric consulting firm that has expanded into exam delivery technology. Founded by former test publisher executives, Caveon is uniquely positioned as a test security authority rather than a traditional assessment platform vendor.

### Core Products
- **Scorpion** – Web-based exam delivery platform
- **SmartItem™** – Proprietary AI-powered item generation technology
- **Web Patrol** – Brain dump and exam fraud detection service
- **Test Validation Studies** – Consulting service for detecting compromised exams

### AI Item Generation
Caveon's SmartItem™ technology is their flagship differentiator. It uses AI to generate large pools of algorithmically-varied items from a parent item template, making it extremely difficult for candidates to memorize and share exam content. SmartItem™ is particularly effective for high-stakes certification programs concerned about test piracy and brain dumps.

### Proctoring Options
- Live remote proctoring via third-party partnerships
- Caveon does not offer proprietary proctoring; they integrate with proctoring vendors
- AI-automated proctoring available through partnerships

### Offline / Resilient Delivery
- Scorpion is primarily web-based
- No native offline exam delivery capability identified
- Relies on stable internet connectivity

### LMS & System Integrations
- LTI-compatible for LMS embedding
- REST API available for custom integrations
- SAML SSO support

### Security Features
- SmartItem™ item generation to prevent memorization
- Web Patrol monitors internet for leaked exam content
- Psychometric anomaly detection for identifying cheating patterns
- Test Validation Studies identify statistical evidence of cheating

### Target Market
- Certification and licensure organizations (professional credentials)
- High-stakes exam programs with significant security concerns
- Organizations with large item banks susceptible to brain dump sites

### Pricing Model
- Subscription + per-exam fees
- Consulting services priced separately
- Enterprise contracts for large certification bodies

### Key Differentiators vs. Meazure
- Strongest test security posture and psychometric consulting in the market
- SmartItem™ has no direct equivalent at Meazure
- Caveon is a competitor AND potential partner (we proctor some Scorpion-delivered exams)
- Less emphasis on candidate experience, more on security

### Recent Updates / News
- Continued investment in SmartItem™ AI capabilities
- Growing partnerships with proctoring vendors to offer full exam delivery + proctoring

### RFP Positioning Notes
- If RFP asks about AI item generation: Caveon is the benchmark — acknowledge their SmartItem™ lead
- If RFP prioritizes security over candidate experience: Caveon is a strong competitor
- Meazure advantage: End-to-end platform (exam delivery + proctoring) vs. Caveon's need for third-party proctoring

## PLATFORM 2: SURPASS

### Overview
Surpass (by BTL Group) is an enterprise-grade computer-based testing (CBT) platform designed for high-volume, high-stakes assessments. It is widely used by national exam boards, awarding bodies, and professional certification organizations globally.

### Core Products
- **Surpass Platform** – End-to-end assessment authoring, delivery, and reporting
- **SecureClient** – Locked-down desktop application for offline/resilient exam delivery
- **Surpass ItemBank** – Collaborative item authoring and management
- **Surpass Assess** – Marking and scoring workflow

### AI Item Generation
- Surpass has introduced AI-assisted item authoring tools
- AI can suggest item improvements, flag biased language, and assist with translation
- Not as advanced as Caveon SmartItem™ for algorithmic item generation at scale

### Proctoring Options
- Surpass integrates with third-party proctoring vendors
- Native AI proctoring flag in Surpass Assess for marking unusual behavior during delivery
- No proprietary live proctoring offering

### Offline / Resilient Delivery
- **SecureClient is a major differentiator** — supports up to 65,000 concurrent exam sessions
- Full offline delivery capability: exam content downloaded to device, syncs results when connectivity restored
- Ideal for test centers with unreliable internet, remote locations, and large-scale national exams
- Used by national examination boards in Africa, Middle East, and Asia where connectivity is inconsistent

### LMS & System Integrations
- LTI 1.3 integration for LMS platforms (Canvas, Moodle, Blackboard, etc.)
- REST API for candidate management, scheduling, and results
- SAML SSO support
- Integrations with Pearson VUE test centers for delivery

### Security Features
- SecureClient locks down the browser and device during exam
- Item bank versioning and access controls
- Role-based access for authors, reviewers, and administrators
- Audit trails for all item bank changes

### Target Market
- National and regional exam boards (K-12, secondary education)
- Professional awarding bodies (UK/Europe focus)
- Large-scale certification programs with test center delivery
- International development organizations running literacy/skills assessments

### Pricing Model
- Enterprise licensing (volume-based)
- Per-exam fees for delivery
- Implementation and professional services fees for setup

### Key Differentiators vs. Meazure
- SecureClient offline delivery is unmatched — Meazure has no equivalent offline delivery product
- Stronger authoring and item bank capabilities than Meazure's current offering
- More focus on exam board/awarding body segment vs. Meazure's corporate/professional cert focus
- Meazure advantage: Superior remote proctoring, broader proctoring modalities, North American market depth

### Recent Updates / News
- Continued expansion in Sub-Saharan Africa and Southeast Asia markets
- Investment in AI-assisted authoring features
- Partnership expansions with regional test center networks

### RFP Positioning Notes
- If RFP requires offline delivery: Surpass SecureClient is the gold standard — be direct about Meazure's gap
- If RFP is North America corporate/professional cert: Meazure remote proctoring depth is a win
- If RFP asks about item authoring: Surpass has richer native tools; Meazure integrates with authoring partners

### Core Products

- **Collaborative Item Authoring** – Multi-user item creation with workflow and approval

### AI Item Generation
- Cirrus has invested in AI-assisted item authoring
- AI essay grading (automated scoring) is a key differentiator for constructed-response items
- AI can flag items with potential bias or readability issues during authoring

### Proctoring Options
- Cirrus integrates with third-party proctoring vendors including Proctorio and others
- No native proprietary proctoring offering
- Browser lockdown capability built into the platform

### Offline / Resilient Delivery
- Cirrus is cloud-native — no offline delivery capability
- Requires stable internet connectivity
- Progressive Web App (PWA) approach for some resilience features

### LMS & System Integrations
- Deep LTI 1.3 integration — strong Canvas, Moodle, Blackboard support
- REST API for candidate data, results, and scheduling
- SAML 2.0 SSO support
- Integrations with SIS (Student Information Systems) popular in European higher ed

### Security Features
- Browser lockdown during exam delivery
- Item bank access controls and versioning
- Audit logs for authoring workflows
- GDPR-compliant data handling (European data residency)

### Target Market
- European higher education institutions (primary market)
- Professional certification bodies in Europe
- Organizations requiring GDPR-compliant exam delivery
- Institutions prioritizing modern UX and collaborative authoring

### Pricing Model
- SaaS subscription model
- Per-candidate or per-exam pricing tiers
- Volume discounts for large institutions

### Key Differentiators vs. Meazure
- AI essay grading is a significant differentiator for constructed-response assessments
- European market presence and GDPR compliance posture is stronger than Meazure's
- Collaborative authoring workflow is more mature
- Meazure advantage: Remote proctoring depth, North American market relationships, hybrid/live proctoring options

### Recent Updates / News
- Continued AI investment in automated scoring and item generation

- Growing partnerships with European awarding bodies

### RFP Positioning Notes
- If European institution RFP: Cirrus GDPR posture and data residency is hard to beat
- If RFP requires AI essay grading: Flag Cirrus as the benchmark; ask if Meazure's partners cover this need
- North American RFPs: Cirrus has limited presence — Meazure has clear advantage in relationships and support

- **Assessments in a Box** – Proprietary hardware solution for offline/disconnected exam delivery

### AI Item Generation
- Limited AI item generation capabilities
- Some AI-assisted features for item analysis and test construction
- Not a primary differentiator for Excelsoft

### Proctoring Options
- Basic AI proctoring built into the platform
- Limited live proctoring offerings
- Primarily serves markets where in-person testing is the norm

### Offline / Resilient Delivery
- **"Assessments in a Box" is the primary differentiator** — proprietary hardware unit that enables exam delivery without any internet connection
- Device acts as a local server; candidates connect to it via LAN/WiFi
- Designed for rural testing centers, government exams in India, and African markets
- Results synced to central server when connectivity is restored

### LMS & System Integrations
- LTI integration available
- REST API for data exchange
- Integration with Indian government exam systems and portals

### Security Features
- Hardware-based security for the "in a box" deployment

### Target Market
- Indian government and state-level examinations (primary market)
- African national exam boards
- Organizations in bandwidth-constrained environments
- Large-scale government recruitment exams

### Pricing Model
- Hardware + software licensing for "in a box" deployments
- SaaS pricing for cloud-based delivery

### Key Differentiators vs. Meazure
- Hardware-based offline delivery is unique in the market
- Strong positioning in India and African government exam markets

### Recent Updates / News

### RFP Positioning Notes

## PLATFORM 9: PROLYDIAN

### Overview
Prolydian is a certification management and exam delivery platform targeting professional certification bodies, membership associations, and workforce development organizations. Prolydian offers a tiered proctoring model, allowing certification programs to choose from unproctored, AI-proctored, or live-proctored exam delivery within a single platform.

### Core Products
- **Prolydian Platform** – Certification management and exam delivery
- **Tiered Proctoring** – Unproctored, AI-proctored, and live-proctored options
- **Certification Management** – Credential lifecycle, renewal, and verification
- **Candidate Portal** – Self-service scheduling and status tracking

### AI Item Generation
- Basic AI-assisted item review
- No advanced AI item generation capability

### Proctoring Options
- **Multi-tier proctoring is Prolydian's key differentiator:**
  - Tier 1: Unproctored (low-stakes, practice, or formative)
  - Tier 2: AI-automated proctoring (mid-stakes)
  - Tier 3: Live human proctoring (high-stakes certification)
- All tiers managed within a single platform workflow

### Offline / Resilient Delivery
- Cloud-based only
- No offline delivery capability

### LMS & System Integrations
- REST API for data exchange
- SAML SSO support
- Integration with AMS (Association Management Systems) used by professional associations
- Zapier/iPaaS connectors for workflow automation

### Security Features
- Tiered security matched to stakes level
- AI proctoring with flagging and human review queue
- Secure browser lockdown for higher-stakes tiers

### Target Market
- Professional certification bodies and associations
- Workforce development and apprenticeship programs
- Trade associations with certification programs
- Organizations transitioning from paper-based to digital certification

### Pricing Model
- Platform subscription + per-exam fees
- Proctoring fees vary by tier
- Volume discounts for large certification programs

### Key Differentiators vs. Meazure
- All-in-one certification management + exam delivery + proctoring in one platform
- Tiered proctoring model gives clients flexibility without multi-vendor complexity
- Meazure advantage: Deeper proctoring expertise and quality (ProctorU/Examity reputation), stronger live proctoring at scale, better for high-stakes high-volume certification programs, existing proctor workforce

### Recent Updates / News
- Growing adoption in trade association certification market
- New AMS integrations for association-based certification programs
- AI proctoring capability enhancements

### RFP Positioning Notes
- Direct competitor in the professional certification space
- If RFP wants all-in-one: Prolydian is a genuine alternative — compete on proctoring quality and scale
- Meazure's ProctorU brand reputation for live proctoring quality is a differentiator
- If RFP separates exam platform from proctoring: Prolydian may still use Meazure for proctoring (partner opportunity)
