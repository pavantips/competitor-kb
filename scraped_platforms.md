## Test Development and Supporting Services

### Our technology and psychometric experts can undertake or support you with:

![Image 18](https://i0.wp.com/surpass.com/wp-content/uploads/services_checkmark.png?fit=300%2C177&ssl=1)

#### Job Task Analysis

We identify the essential knowledge, skills, and abilities required for specific roles, providing you with a relevant and concise exam blueprint.

![Image 19](https://i0.wp.com/surpass.com/wp-content/uploads/services_checkmark.png?fit=300%2C177&ssl=1)

#### Test Specification / Exam Blueprint Design

Using job task analysis data, our team will help you develop and validate the test specifications and exam blueprint according to your program goals and industry expectations. Then, with our Tasks solution in Surpass, we can help you manage the item writing process with assigned tasks, quotas and deadlines. A perfect solution to ensure your SMEs’ efforts map to the blueprint.

![Image 20](https://i0.wp.com/surpass.com/wp-content/uploads/services_checkmark.png?fit=300%2C177&ssl=1)

#### Subject Matter Expert (SME) Recruitment

Finding the right team to author your items is crucial. Our team can help you, using both psychometric expertise and innovative technology. Surpass also enhances the retention and monitoring of SMEs; our item development tools are easy to use, with no lengthy training needed, and the data generated from authoring and test performance can help you refine your recruitment process.

![Image 21](https://i0.wp.com/surpass.com/wp-content/uploads/services_checkmark.png?fit=300%2C177&ssl=1)

#### Item Writing

Our team assists in crafting high-quality test items, offering both in-person and virtual workshops to develop effective multiple-choice questions and innovative item .

Explore our built-in assistive AI feature-set, [Surpass Copilot.](https://surpass.com/copilot/) which enables you to generate new items using AI, streamlining the item authoring process and saving valuable time.

![Image 22](https://i0.wp.com/surpass.com/wp-content/uploads/services_checkmark.png?fit=300%2C177&ssl=1)

#### Advanced Item Creation Training

With Surpass, it’s easy to integrate your existing item bank and use this as a starting point for creating new advanced items.

As well as our award-winning high-fidelity image viewer, you will have access to functionality such as spreadsheet simulations, audio capture question types and in-test video.

![Image 23](https://i0.wp.com/surpass.com/wp-content/uploads/services_checkmark.png?fit=300%2C177&ssl=1)

#### Item Banking

With Surpass, your item bank belongs to you. At every stage in the process, you are in control of your item bank with the freedom to edit, import, or export items whenever you wish. With extensive metadata management solutions and the ability to create comprehensive item lists, our item banking solution is all you need regardless of whether you are delivering via Surpass, paper, or another third-party testing vendor.

![Image 24](https://i0.wp.com/surpass.com/wp-content/uploads/services_checkmark.png?fit=300%2C177&ssl=1)

#### Standard Setting

We guide you through establishing appropriate passing scores using methodologies best suited to your program, facilitating the process either in-person or virtually.

![Image 25](https://i0.wp.com/surpass.com/wp-content/uploads/services_checkmark.png?fit=300%2C177&ssl=1)

#### Test maintenance

Our services include ongoing statistical analyses to ensure your test scores remain reliable and valid, with customized maintenance plans tailored to your program’s needs.

![Image 26](https://i0.wp.com/surpass.com/wp-content/uploads/services_checkmark.png?fit=300%2C177&ssl=1)

#### Complex psychometrics

Our psychometricians specialize in advanced methodologies, including Item Response Theory (IRT), Computer Adaptive Testing (CAT), and Structural Equation Modeling (SEM), among others.

![Image 27](https://i0.wp.com/surpass.com/wp-content/uploads/services_checkmark.png?fit=300%2C177&ssl=1)

#### Test production and publishing

Our experts can work with you to design your exam blueprint and content, test form plan, test delivery frequency, test delivery models, and administration specification. Surpass is the market-leader for the instant publishing of items; questions in your Surpass item bank can be instantly included or withdrawn from tests.

![Image 28](https://i0.wp.com/surpass.com/wp-content/uploads/services_checkmark.png?fit=300%2C177&ssl=1)

#### Item-level and Test-level analysis

Often described as the science of testing, our experts will work with you to identify the best approach (CTT or IRT) to undertake ongoing statistical maintenance of a test. Surpass procedures involve analyzing item and test-level data for security concerns, drift, bias / DIF, and consistent pass rates. Our expertise will underpin your confidence in the validity of your test results and that they meet your program design objectives and requirements.

Alternatively, if you prefer to handle the data yourself, Surpass allows a raw data extraction, so you’re never tied to the system.

## Product Customization

### Get custom features added to the Surpass Platform

It is often the case that making the decision to buy/license an existing assessment platform or build your own often comes down to how your requirements differ from market trends. Surpass is customer-driven; our roadmap is influenced by the needs of the market, but these don’t always match all the needs of our customer base. We have therefore implemented a Sponsored Development Team model which has successfully proven to provide customers and partners the ability to build bespoke features which bring value to their market and business growth.

Because we operate an agile approach to development, additional teams sponsored by our customers and partners can create features within Surpass, giving you the option to build what matters to you on top of an already excellent product. It’s better than an Open Source approach, because all development is supported and quality checked by professionals, so everyone benefits without the uncontrollable risks of Open Source development.
